# Chessy Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Developer / Data Controller: Chessy: Chess Puzzles
Contact Email: chessy.appstore@outlook.com

This Privacy Policy explains how Chessy ("Chessy," the "App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the Chessy mobile application.

Chessy is a single-player chess game with puzzle and classic modes. The current version does not require account creation or include social features. The App may display Google AdMob advertisements and offers iOS in-app purchases processed through Apple and managed in-app through RevenueCat.

## 1. Information We Process

We process only the information reasonably needed to operate the App, save your progress, support in-app purchases, and display or limit ads. Depending on how you use the App, this may include:

- Puzzle progress data, such as your current level, score, stars, completed levels, move-related state, hint state, sound setting, haptics setting, and related timestamps.
- Classic mode progress data, such as your selected difficulty, match outcomes, path unlocks, free-play preferences, and related timestamps.
- Device-stored preferences and technical state needed to keep the App working on your device.
- Optional iCloud-synced puzzle progression and a RevenueCat purchase identity value when iCloud is enabled and available for the App on your Apple device.
- In-app purchase and restore data processed through Apple and RevenueCat, such as product identifiers, entitlement status, purchase status, transaction identifiers, transaction dates, restore results, and internal grant records used to prevent duplicate star grants.
- Advertising and consent-related data processed by Google Mobile Ads / Google User Messaging Platform, such as consent choices, ad request metadata, limited device or app identifiers, and rewarded-ad completion status.
- Information you choose to send to us directly outside the App, such as your email address and the contents of your support message.

## 2. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect Through the App

In the current version of Chessy, we do not intentionally collect the following through the App itself:

- Your name, phone number, postal address, or government-issued identifier.
- Your contacts, photos, camera data, precise location, health data, or microphone recordings.
- Your payment card or bank account details. Apple handles payment processing for App Store and in-app purchase transactions.
- A username/password account for Chessy, because the App does not currently operate its own user account system.
- Personal information for marketing profiles or behavioral profiling by us directly.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above only to:

- Provide gameplay and core app functionality.
- Save local progress and preferences on your device.
- Sync eligible puzzle progression and purchase identity data across your Apple devices through iCloud when available.
- Process, validate, restore, and honor in-app purchases through Apple and RevenueCat.
- Apply purchased stars, prevent duplicate purchase grants, and honor permanent ad-removal purchases.
- Request, display, measure, limit, or disable advertisements, including rewarded ads that can unlock gameplay hints.
- Store and honor privacy or ad consent choices where required.
- Troubleshoot, secure, maintain, and improve the reliability of the App.
- Respond to support requests you send to us.

## 4. Where Data Is Stored and Who Processes It

Chessy currently uses the following storage and service providers:

- Local device storage: The App stores gameplay progress, settings, and purchase-grant state locally on your device.
- Apple iCloud key-value storage: Eligible puzzle progress and the RevenueCat app user identifier may also sync through your personal iCloud account if iCloud is enabled for the App on supported Apple devices.
- Apple App Store / in-app purchase infrastructure: Apple processes App Store and in-app purchase transactions under Apple's own terms and privacy disclosures.
- RevenueCat: RevenueCat helps us manage purchase state, entitlements, restore flows, and transaction-based grant protection for in-app purchases.
- Google Mobile Ads / Google User Messaging Platform: Google may process consent and ad-related data to deliver banner, interstitial, or rewarded ads, depending on your eligibility and settings.

Important notes:

- Puzzle progress may sync through iCloud.
- Classic mode progress is currently stored locally on the device only and is not synced to iCloud in the current version.
- The embedded chess engine used in classic mode runs locally on the device. In the current version, your moves are not sent to our servers for analysis.
- We do not operate our own dedicated gameplay backend for Chessy at this time.

## 5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited data only in the following situations:

- With Apple, when you use iCloud-enabled sync features, download the App from the App Store, or make App Store / in-app purchase transactions.
- With RevenueCat, to manage in-app purchase status, entitlements, restore flows, and duplicate-grant protection.
- With Google and its advertising or consent service providers to request, measure, deliver, limit, or disable ads and to manage consent signals.
- If required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or a valid government request.
- If reasonably necessary to protect our rights, users, or the security of the App.

When you use Apple services, Google advertising services, or RevenueCat purchase services, those providers may process data under their own terms and privacy policies. We do not receive your Apple ID credentials or your payment card details.

## 6. Data Retention

We keep data only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this Policy:

- Local app data generally remains on your device until you delete the App, clear your device data, or the data is overwritten during normal app use.
- iCloud-synced puzzle progress or purchase identity data may remain in your personal iCloud account until you disable iCloud for the App or remove the related data through Apple account or device settings.
- Purchase metadata, entitlement status, and transaction records handled by Apple or RevenueCat may be retained under those providers' own retention policies.
- Support communications, if any, may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to you, maintain records, or comply with legal obligations.

## 7. Your Privacy Choices

You can manage your data in the following ways:

- You can stop using the App at any time.
- You can delete the App to remove locally stored data from that device, subject to your operating system's behavior.
- You can disable iCloud access for Chessy in your Apple device settings if you do not want eligible puzzle progress or purchase identity data to sync through iCloud.
- You can use the App's restore-purchases flow to restore eligible non-consumable purchase access, such as ad removal, on supported iOS devices with the same Apple account.
- You can manage your Apple purchase settings and billing directly through Apple.
- Where shown, you can review or update ad consent choices through the in-app consent flow or applicable platform settings.
- You can limit ad tracking through Apple device privacy settings, including App Tracking Transparency controls where available.

Because we do not operate a dedicated Chessy account system or our own gameplay backend, we generally cannot directly access or delete data that exists only on your device, in your personal iCloud account, or in Apple-managed purchase systems.

If you contact us directly and want us to delete support communications we hold, contact us at chessy.appstore@outlook.com.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Chessy is a general-audience game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App itself.

If you believe a child has sent us personal information directly, contact us at chessy.appstore@outlook.com and we will review the request.

## 9. International Processing

If you use iCloud, App Store purchases, RevenueCat-managed purchase features, or Google AdMob services, Apple, RevenueCat, and Google may process data using systems located in countries other than your own. Those activities are governed by their own legal terms and privacy disclosures.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make a material change, we will update the "Last updated" date above and may provide additional notice where required by law.
